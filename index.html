Know Bhutan

Escape to a land untouched by time: Bhutan. This unique kingdom of Bhutan offers a chance to experience a living tapestry of culture and religion. Hike through ancient monasteries clinging to cliffsides, where monks in saffron robes chant and share their wisdom. Immerse yourself in the philosophy of Gross National Happiness.Bhutan promises an unforgettable encounter with a culture unlike any other.
