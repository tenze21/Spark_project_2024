Certificate in

Sustainability in the World of AI Technology

This course explores the intersection of artificial intelligence (AI) and sustainability, focusing on how AI can be harnessed to address environmental challenges and promote sustainable practices. Participants will learn about the environmental impact of AI technologies, including energy consumption of large-scale data centres and the lifecycle of AI hardware.

Course Outline

1/6

Week 1 : Introduction to AI and Sustainability

Understand intersection of AI and sustainability with ethical considerations in AI development.

Case studies: AI applications in environmental conservation, climate modelling, resource optimization

Certificate in

Generative Digital Media

This course offers an in-depth exploration of generative digital media, focusing on the use of algorithms and artificial intelligence to create and manipulate digital content. Students will engage with a variety of techniques and tools used to generate images, video, and audio content autonomously.

Course Outline

1/6

Week 1 : Introduction to AI & Digital Media Design

Explore application of digital media technology in AI and its impact on various aspects of life.

Learn transformation of visual art including creative reconstruction and production of new media art materials.

Certificate in

AI for Business

This dynamic course introduces the applications of artificial intelligence (AI) in various business sectors, focusing on how AI can drive innovation, streamline operations, and enhance decision-making processes. Participants will explore the fundamentals of AI technology, including machine learning, natural language processing, and robotics, and how these can be leveraged to solve real-world business problems.

Course Outline

1/4

Week 1 : Introduction to AI and Sustainability

Understand intersection of AI and sustainability with ethical considerations in AI development.

Case studies: AI applications in environmental conservation, climate modelling, resource optimization
