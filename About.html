What is GCIT?

Gyalpozhing College of Information Technology (GCIT), affiliated with the Royal University of Bhutan, was inaugurated in 2017 and offers a modern approach to information technology education. The college emphasizes practical and project-based learning, with a strong focus on cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, blockchain, and full-stack development.

Campus Life

The campus atmosphere is vibrant, with a strong emphasis on both academic excellence and holistic development. Students can participate in a variety of tech-focused groups and events, such as hackathons, coding competitions, and workshops on emerging technologies. These activities not only enhance their technical skills but also promote teamwork and problem-solving abilities.

Faculty

GCIT's faculty comprises a diverse mix of local and foreign experts, enhancing the educational experience with a blend of international perspectives and local insights. This team is dedicated to fostering a learning environment that emphasizes industry-relevant skills and global standards.
